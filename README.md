Schedule Optimizer is an AI-powered mobile app designed for high school students who want to take control of their academic workload. Unlike traditional planners, it **learns from your feedback** to provide increasingly accurate time estimates and personalized study plans.

### The Problem
High school students often struggle with:
- Underestimating how long assignments take
- Procrastinating until deadlines approach
- Ineffective study sessions without proper breaks
- Managing multiple classes with varying difficulty levels

### The Solution
Schedule Optimizer uses adaptive AI to:
- Predict assignment completion times based on your past performance
- Generate realistic, personalized study schedules
- Track your progress and continuously improve predictions
- Provide structured study sessions with timed breaks

## ğŸ§  How the AI Works

The app uses a feedback loop to continuously improve:

1. **Initial Prediction**: AI analyzes assignment description and difficulty
2. **User Completion**: You mark the assignment done and input actual time
3. **Learning**: AI compares prediction vs reality and adjusts its model
4. **Improvement**: Next predictions incorporate your specific patterns

This creates a **personalized AI** that understands YOUR working speed, not just generic averages.
